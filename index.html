Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our process

How long does the custom home building process typically take?

The timeline varies based on the complexity and size of the project, but typically ranges from 10 to 18 months. This includes design development, permitting, construction, and final finishes. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is the typical budget range for a custom home?

Custom homes vary significantly based on location, size, materials, and finishes. Our projects typically range from $500 per square foot for standard finishes to $1,000+ per square foot for luxury specifications. We work within your budget to maximize value and quality.

Do you work with clients' architects or interior designers?

Yes, we frequently collaborate with our clients' chosen architects and designers. Our team has experience working with top design professionals to ensure a cohesive vision and seamless execution from concept to completion.

What makes Arcadia Homes different from other custom builders?

Our 20+ years of experience, commitment to craftsmanship, and personalized approach set us apart. We limit the number of projects we take on each year to ensure each client receives our full attention and the highest level of service throughout the entire process.
